Covid 19 and Consumer Mobility

In order to narrow the scope of the project, we seleced Texas as the geographic region of focus. A public, anonymized data set maintained by Apple Inc provided population mobility information for 3 modes; driving, transit, and walking. With daily records for each county, we mapped these values as a time series to discover trends.

  • Relative mobility was used to control for the deviations that would normally be seen across varying population sizes.
  • We saw mobility proportionately respond to trends in Covid cases, coupled with state closures. In turn, we also saw mobility patterns beginning to rebound to normal levels, only to fall again in line with a resurgence of cases and government response.
