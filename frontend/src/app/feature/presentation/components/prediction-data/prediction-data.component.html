Application of Amazon Forecast AI Model

The forecasted data can be used to infer upcoming mobility trends within counties, which we have found generally correlated with retail sales for certain industries. Forecasted relative mobility is predicted at the county level based on its time series driving data. Trained on the DeepAR algorithm with a day frequency rate, the prediction model reflects increases in mobility affected by weekends, seasonality, and responses to the changing COVID restrictions

Three prediction quantiles - p[0.1], p[0.5], p[0.9] - are displayed to indicate the level of uncertainty in each forecast. To determine which to use, consider the costs of under/over-estimation (e.g. for the p[0.1] prediction, the true value is expected to be the lower than the predicted value 10% of the time)

Results have an average 4% error in either direction. Future improvements include addition of associated COVID time series data and county metadata (economic, demographic).
